Dear Eomma,

I write this letter because I am told to write it. Actually I was told about your birthday and what had happened. If I was in your place I would have complained to my mom and dad about the party and told my cousins. I don’t know how you could handle that and be patient about it. To me you’re ideal when it comes to waiting and having patience for someone that isn’t me. It is why I look up to you for being a great mother figure. You always do the best you can and sometimes people underestimate you. I hate it when people do that. That is why I try to copy you whenever you do something or say something. I want people to know just how amazing you are because you taught me a lot of things and even repeat it over and over again because my brain doesn’t even go through with what you say.

The fact you deal with me is enough for me to admire you as a woman. I someday want to be like you, strong, dependable, understanding, and GOD THAT PATIENCE OF YOURS. It is something I want to accomplish for sure because I know I don’t have patience for anyone when something is going on. I unfortunately got that from spending time with Appa. Anger issues are something I’m struggling with, but it makes me happy that you are always there for me. I remember that time when me and Appa had an argument. It was really bad that I started to guilt him, which worked for a bit until you caught me doing that. You scolded me for a solid 2 hours because of the method I was using. It made me feel bad but you were telling me that for a reason. The way you scolded me did make me sad, but I understood why you did it. It made me feel oddly happy because My IRL mom isn’t the best and ignores me all the time. For a moment it felt like you were my mom. It was nice because It was a moment that I wanted to have a mother around.

You also would read me stories when Zero and Calix were around. I would stick around just to listen and then I would fall asleep too. It was actually sweet because when you kept reading I was able to feel safe with your voice, it’s something about your voice Mom. I like that you made me feel safe. When you were reading the Cultivation story I would fall asleep and when I woke up you were still in call but you were unmuted. I remember I was about to type in the text chat only to realize you fell asleep while reading to me and the others. You were asleep and you kept reading even if we woke up in the middle of the night. I like to think you were asleep for more because you stood up for us past 2 AM. That was the memory I held closely to me even until now. Mom, I am happy to have met you and I hope I can make you happy with my achievements in the future. I wish you a happy birthday!. I will be a good banana and not be in trouble anymore this year! I promise to watch just like mama watched.

From,
Your banana.

Honestly, that allergy made my lips smooth af, and my makeup still slayed during that whole experience.

I digress!! From the four or three years that we spent, thank you for being my friend and mother. These memories we made, I cherish them the most. Truly, you are one of the greatest experiences and influences in my life that keeps me going through this journey.

Happy 21st birthday, momm!!
Sincerely, Froglet
